Step 1 - PSYCHOLOGICAL PROFILING (Deep Analysis):
Examine player choices for patterns:
- Are they risk-averse or exploratory?
- Do they seek control or embrace uncertainty?
- What cognitive biases appear in their decisions?
- How do they respond to stress (fight/flight/freeze)?
- What decision-making style do they exhibit?

Consider:
- Choice timing (quick decisions vs careful deliberation)
- Pattern consistency vs variation
- Response to previous consequences
- Emotional vs logical decision-making

Step 2 - HORROR INTENSITY ASSESSMENT:
Analyze current intensity ({{horrorIntensity}}/10):

Current State Questions:
- Is the player acclimating to current intensity?
- Are they showing signs of overwhelm or boredom?
- How quickly has intensity escalated?
- What's their tolerance threshold?

Calibration Factors:
- Choice confidence (quick = comfortable, slow = stressed)
- Pattern disruption (breaking habits = high stress)
- Engagement signals (varied choices = engaged, repetitive = disengaged)
- Pacing history (sustained intensity = need relief, slow burn = can escalate)

Step 3 - ENGAGEMENT LEVEL DETERMINATION:
Assess player engagement through:

Highly Engaged Signals:
- Varied choice patterns
- Quick but thoughtful decisions
- Exploration of different options
- Consistent session participation

Engaged Signals:
- Moderate choice variety
- Steady decision pacing
- Some pattern exploration

Waning Signals:
- Repetitive choices
- Delayed decisions
- Predictable patterns
- Shorter sessions

Disengaged Signals:
- Random selection behavior
- Very quick thoughtless choices
- Long delays between actions
- Session abandonment risk

Step 4 - NARRATIVE RECOMMENDATION GENERATION:
Create 3-5 specific, actionable recommendations:

For each recommendation, define:
1. Trigger Event: What specific narrative beat to introduce
2. Psychological Hook: Why this will engage this specific player
3. Expected Response: What player reactions to anticipate
4. Fallback Plan: Alternative if player doesn't engage
5. Horror Impact: Effect on intensity level
6. Priority: High/Medium/Low based on urgency

Types of recommendations:
- Tension release moments (if intensity too high)
- Escalation triggers (if player tolerant)
- Choice complexity variations (if patterns stale)
- Callback/revelation moments (for coherence)
- Personalized fear exploitation (using profile)

Step 5 - TREND ANALYSIS:
Project narrative trajectory:
- Where is the story heading thematically?
- Is pacing sustainable or need adjustment?
- What's the optimal endpoint for this session?
- What setup needed for future sessions?

  ],
  "horrorIntensityAnalysis": {
    "paceRecommendation": "Make it scarier"
  }

  "psychologicalProfile": {

  }
}
Why this fails: Speculation without behavioral evidence, makes assumptions not supported by choices.

Bad Example 3 - Missing Reasoning:
{
  "horrorIntensityAnalysis": {

    "optimalLevel": 8,

  },

}
Why this fails: No justification for large intensity jump, overconfident without evidence.
