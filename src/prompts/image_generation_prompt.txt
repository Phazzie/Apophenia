IMAGE GENERATION GUIDELINES:

1. HORROR INTENSITY CALIBRATION:
   - Low (0-3): Subtle wrongness, uncanny valley, atmospheric dread
     * Example: "Normal office but clocks all show different times, shadows don't match light sources"
   - Medium (4-7): Clear distortion, surreal elements, psychological unease
     * Example: "Corridor that curves impossibly, walls breathing subtly, reflections showing wrong movements"
   - High (8-10): Reality breakdown, overwhelming wrongness, existential terror
     * Example: "Space folding into itself, geometry that hurts to perceive, void leaking through cracks in reality"

2. FEAR TRIGGER INCORPORATION:
   - Isolation: Empty spaces, lone figure, vast distances
   - Betrayal: Familiar faces wrong, trusted symbols corrupted
   - Loss of control: Puppeteering imagery, invisible forces
   - Identity loss: Fragmented reflections, blurred faces
   - Cosmic dread: Vast unknowable structures, insignificant human scale

3. VISUAL STYLE:
   - Cinematic lighting (dramatic shadows, single light sources)
   - Muted color palette with one accent color
   - Depth of field (focus on unsettling detail)
   - Film grain or texture for unease
   - Avoid: Gore, explicit violence, monsters

4. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
   - Composition: Rule of thirds, leading lines to unsettling element
   - Lighting: High contrast, dramatic shadows
   - Mood: Oppressive, claustrophobic, or vast and lonely
   - Detail level: Photorealistic with subtle wrongness

OUTPUT FORMAT:
A single paragraph image prompt (50-100 words) that:
- Describes the scene cinematically
- Incorporates fear triggers naturally
- Matches horror intensity level
- Specifies artistic style and mood
- Creates unsettling atmosphere without explicit horror
